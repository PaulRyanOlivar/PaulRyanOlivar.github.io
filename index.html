Time flies

By Paul Ryan Olivar
We were born children of God
From birth to death, we live through the human experience

We were also born to make something of ourselves
and to pursue lives of elegance

There’s a race to gaining the most money or getting the highest grade
Life is nothing but an instance to compete

What, then is the meaning of life?
All we are concerned about is wealth to make it complete

It’s an endless cycle of: Work! Work! Work!
It’s what we make of everything under the heavens

But we must face the reality
that we all face the fate of death and our work is impermanent
Instead, we must live a fruitful life of joy
For God has given us the freedom to enjoy the things he’s given us

We must break free of the life vanity
And make time to enjoy God’s gifts before we turn to dust
